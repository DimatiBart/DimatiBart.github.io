Secure flight info

The United States Department of Homeland Security (DHS) have announced that Secure Flight Passenger Data (SFPD) must be entered into all flight bookings for passengers travelling to the USA.
STA Travel request this information from all passengers flying to, from, within or transiting the United States. This information is vital in order for us to pass onto the airlines and ensure that your flights are not cancelled or that you are refused boarding at the airport.

WHAT DO WE REQUIRE?

  • Full name as it appears in your passport (including middle name)
  • Date of Birth
  • Redress Number if applicable (this is a unique number provided by the Department of Homeland Security to individuals who have similar names to known threats. Only passengers who already have a Redress Number will be required to provide this.)

For further information, please visit www.tsa.gov.
